About Us

The Beginning

We are a fine restorante built from the heart of Italy itself. Owner Adolfo Abele was born and reared in Florence, Italy till the age 17. His grandmother Elenora Agapito used to cook and serve food in the town square. Elenora's son or Adolfos father Adalberto wanted to Adolfo to stay in the town square and sell bowls of noodles to the people. At 17 yearsof age Adolfo felt like he was missing something in life and decided to become a kungfu ninja. He fought glorious battles and gained the respect of his ninja master Pinkanoob in the great Apennine Mountainss. After Adoflo reached enlightment by age 32, he decided to go back and help his family back in Italy selling noodles. However, on his journey home he had a vision of opening a an Italian Restorante in America.

Today

Now at 50 and strong, Adolfo welcomes all his friends and family into a piece of Italy in his backyard.

I welcome anyone to come and have a small piece of Italy.

They serve a big variety of homemade dishes that you would think your grandmother cooked herself. Nothing is complete without fine wine to wash down the flavors of Florence.
